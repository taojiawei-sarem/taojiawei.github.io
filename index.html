Biography

I am currently a direct Ph.D. student in Electronic Science and Technology at Nanjing University of Science and Technology. I received my B.Eng. degree in Microelectronic Science and Engineering from Nanjing University of Science and Technology in 2025, ranking 1st out of 31 students.

My research interests include synthetic aperture radar (SAR) imaging, radar jamming imaging and characterization, electromagnetic scattering modeling, scattering-center analysis, and physics-inspired deep learning for radar and remote sensing.
